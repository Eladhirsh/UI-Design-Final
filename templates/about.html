About NYC Foody

NYC Foody was created by Elad and Omer, two friends fascinated by New York City's vibrant food culture. From artisanal pizzas in Brooklyn to authentic dumplings in Queens, they wanted to build an interactive guide that celebrates each borough's iconic dishes and culinary history.
